Crêpes

Why this recipe :

I love making crêpes since my chilwood!

Flipping the crêpes in the pan was the highlight of a lot of sunday afternoon.

This is a recipe that I wrote on a piece of paper because I was scared to lose the perfect proportions and the appropriate order for the ingredients

During the Covid-19 lockdown, I perfected my ability to make them thin and light

Now it's here ... wherever it is !

You'll need :

  • 250 g
    Flour
  • ½ tsp
    Salt
  • 2 Tbsp
    Sugar
  • 4
    Eggs
  • 500 mL
    Milk
  • 50 g
    Melted Butter
  • Some
    Brown Sugar
  • 2
    Lemons

Instructions :

  1. Mix the flour, salt and sugar in a mixing bowl

  2. Add the eggs and start mixing with a whisker

  3. Add the milk slowly while mixing

  4. Add the melted butter in it and put in a fridge for 1hour minimum

  5. Heat the flat pan with a little bit of butter

  6. Put the fire to medium heat and put the liquid in a pan 1 minute

  7. Unstick the edges of the crêpe and flip it

  8. Let it cook on the other side 1 minute

  9. Put it in a plate

  10. Put a bit more of butter in the pan and do it again until there is no more liquid in the mixing bowl

  11. Serve immediately with a teaspoon of brown sugar and 5 drops of lemon juice
